## Positioning Implications

**Honest framing:**
- "Based on validated neuroscience principles"
- "Clinical application of established research"
- "Awaiting formal outcome studies"

**NOT claiming:**
- "Scientifically proven"
- "Research-validated protocol"
- "Evidence-based" (in the formal sense)

**The truth:** This is sophisticated clinical synthesis of validated mechanisms, not an independently tested intervention. The components work in isolation; the specific combination and protocol await outcome research.
