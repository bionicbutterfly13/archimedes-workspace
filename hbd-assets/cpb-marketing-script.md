## CPB Blocks (1-20)

### CPB 01 — System Graveyard
**Claim:** This works even if you feel like you have a graveyard of systems that worked for two weeks and then got abandoned like everything else.

**Proof Point:** The Replay Loop Breaker helps you recognize the exact moment your system decides it is safer to quit than continue, and interrupts that decision before you act on it.

**Benefits:**

- **Experiential:** You're editing a client proposal, hit a sticking point, and feel the urge to switch tasks. You notice the urge, pause, and finish the proposal instead.
- **Emotional:** You feel accomplished because the temptation showed up and you followed through anyway.
